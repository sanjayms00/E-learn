How Elearn Works

Register/Login

Users can create an account or log in to access the platform's features. Registration allows users to personalize their learning experience and track their progress.

Select the Course

Once logged in, users can browse through a wide range of courses available on the platform. They can search for courses based on their interests, level of expertise, or specific topics.

Pay for the Course

After selecting a course, users can proceed to payment. The platform offers secure payment options, ensuring a seamless transaction process.

Stream the Course

Upon successful payment, users can start streaming the course content immediately. The platform provides high-quality video lectures, interactive quizzes, and supplementary materials to enhance the learning experience.

Track the Progress

Users can track their progress through the course curriculum. The platform keeps track of completed lessons, quizzes, and assignments, allowing users to pick up where they left off.

Review the Course

After completing the course, users have the opportunity to leave a review and rating based on their experience. This feedback helps other users make informed decisions when choosing courses.

Chat with Instructors

Throughout the course, users can engage with course instructors and fellow learners through chat features. They can ask questions, seek clarification, and participate in discussions to deepen their understanding of the course material.

Lifetime Access to the Course

Gain unlimited access to course materials for a lifetime. Learn at your own pace, revisit content whenever needed, and stay updated with new materials at no extra cost. Our commitment to lifelong learning ensures that you have the flexibility and resources to achieve your educational goals effectively.
